## ⚙️ Configuration & Setup

Before running the application, you need to configure your local PostgreSQL database connection. 

Open the `src/main/resources/META-INF/persistence.xml` file and update the following properties with your local database credentials:

```xml
<properties>
            <property name="jakarta.persistence.jdbc.url"
                      value="jdbc:postgresql://localhost:5432/u4_buildweek"/>
            <property name="jakarta.persistence.jdbc.user" value="postgres"/>
            <property name="jakarta.persistence.jdbc.password" value="1234"/>
            <property name="jakarta.persistence.jdbc.driver" value="org.postgresql.Driver"/>
            <property name="hibernate.hbm2ddl.auto" value="update"/>
        </properties>
